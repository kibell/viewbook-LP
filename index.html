At SNHU we believe in hands on learning experiences

You’re not just sitting in a lecture hall listening. You’re learning by doing. With programs design to prepare you for your career. You’ll develop real-world skills and the confidence to go out and achieve your goals.

School of Arts, Sciences and Education

From studying cells under the microscope in a lab to enjoying the 24 acres of trails in our on-campus arboretum, learning at SNHU isn't stuck in a textbook.

Here, you might find yourself student teaching in an elementary classroom or workshopping a social ad with your peers. If breaking down case studies and analyzing crime scenes is more up your alley, we do that, too.

School of Business

Imagine interning with a local sports team, answering tax prep questions from community members or conducting business simulations where you'll learn how to start, operate, manage and grow a coffee shop.

Through group case studies, our sales immersion lab and on-campus competitions, you can build the real-life experience employers are looking for.

School of Science, Technology and Aeronautics

Create new worlds in virtual reality or 3D print a prosthetic limb in this one. If you can dream it, you can build it.

Here, you'll put theory into practice on a daily basis. Through collaborating with peers, faculty and the community, you can solve real-world problems for clients (or even yourself).

With a drone-flying arena, unmanned aerial vehicle prep space, woodshop, robotics lab and more, you'll have all the tools at hand to bring your ideas off the page.

Student support like no other

We surround each student with a constellation of support that includes academics and advising, accessibility, career services, nutrition  and health and wellness including counseling. At SNHU, you’re one of us — and you never have to face any situation alone.

SNHU celebrates our first-generation college students!

Since 1932, Southern New Hampshire University's mission has been to expand access to high-quality, affordable education. In fact, many of our faculty and staff are first-generation college students!

If neither of your parents nor anyone who raised you has graduated with a bachelor's degree, you’re a first-generation college student — and we’re thrilled you’re applying to SNHU!
